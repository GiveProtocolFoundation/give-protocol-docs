## Choose Your Role

Give Protocol supports three primary user types. Choose the path that best fits you:

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Donor** | Individuals who want to make charitable donations using cryptocurrency |
| **Charity / Organization** | Non-profits and verified organizations seeking to receive donations |
| **Volunteer** | Individuals offering time and skills to registered organizations |

## Step 1: Create an Account

1. Visit [app.giveprotocol.io](https://app.giveprotocol.io)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email address via the confirmation link

## Step 2: Connect Your Wallet

Give Protocol uses Web3 wallet authentication for blockchain transactions.

**Supported wallets:**
- MetaMask
- WalletConnect-compatible wallets
- Coinbase Wallet

To connect:
1. Click **Connect Wallet** in the top navigation
2. Select your wallet provider
3. Approve the connection request in your wallet
4. Your wallet address is now linked to your account

## Step 3: Complete Your Profile

Fill in your profile information:
- Display name
- Profile photo (optional)
- Bio / description
- Location (optional)

## Step 4: Choose Your Path

### If you are a Donor

- Browse the [Discover](/platform-features/search-discovery/) page to find causes
- No additional verification required to start donating
- Set up recurring donations or one-time gifts

### If you are an Organization

- Navigate to **Register Organization** in your account settings
- Submit your organization details and legal documents
- Our team reviews your application (typically 3–5 business days)
- Once verified, your organization page goes live

### If you are a Volunteer

- Complete your skill profile
- Browse volunteer opportunities on the platform
- Apply to opportunities and track your hours
