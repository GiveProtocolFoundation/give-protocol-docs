## Welcome to Give Protocol

This guide will walk you through everything you need to get up and running in under 10 minutes.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need:

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- A crypto wallet (MetaMask recommended for beginners)
- A small amount of GLMR (Moonbeam) or DEV (Moonbase Alpha testnet) for gas fees

## Quick Start

### 1. Create Your Account

Go to [app.giveprotocol.io](https://app.giveprotocol.io) and sign up with your email address.

### 2. Connect Your Wallet

Click **Connect Wallet** and follow the prompts. We recommend MetaMask for first-time users.

> **Tip:** If you do not have a wallet yet, visit [metamask.io](https://metamask.io) to create one for free.

### 3. Explore the Platform

Once logged in, you can:

- **Browse charities** on the Discover page
- **Check your dashboard** for an overview of your activity
- **View your profile** and customize it

### 4. Make Your First Donation

1. Find a cause you care about
2. Click **Donate**
3. Enter the amount
4. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
5. Your donation is recorded on the blockchain!
