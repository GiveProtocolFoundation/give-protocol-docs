## Why Your Profile Matters

A complete profile helps organizations and the community recognize your contributions and builds trust within the platform.

## Profile Information

Navigate to **Account** → **Profile** to edit:

- **Display Name**: Your name as shown on the platform
- **Profile Photo**: Upload an image (JPG/PNG, max 2MB)
- **Bio**: A short description of your giving goals or interests
- **Location**: City/Country (optional, shown publicly)
- **Website**: Your personal or organization website (optional)

## Privacy Settings

Control what others can see:
- **Public profile**: Anyone can view your activity summary
- **Private profile**: Only you can see your full history
- **Anonymous donations**: Donate without your name appearing on organization pages

## Notification Preferences

Configure email and push notifications for:
- New campaigns from followed organizations
- Donation receipts and confirmations
- Volunteer application updates
- Platform announcements

## Connected Accounts

Link external accounts (optional):
- GitHub (for developer-focused organizations)
- Twitter/X (for social proof)
