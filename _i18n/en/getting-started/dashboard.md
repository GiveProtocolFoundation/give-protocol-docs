## Your Dashboard

The dashboard is your central hub for monitoring your activity on Give Protocol.

## Dashboard Sections

### Activity Summary

At the top of your dashboard, you will see:

- **Total Donated**: Cumulative amount donated across all organizations
- **Active Organizations**: Organizations you currently support
- **Volunteer Hours**: Total verified volunteer hours
- **Impact Score**: Your combined giving and volunteering score

### Recent Transactions

A chronological list of your recent donations with:
- Organization name
- Amount donated (in crypto and fiat equivalent)
- Transaction hash (links to blockchain explorer)
- Date and time

### My Organizations

Organizations you follow or have donated to. Click any card to:
- View the organization's profile
- See your donation history
- Set up recurring donations

### Upcoming Campaigns

Campaigns from organizations you follow that are currently active or starting soon.

## Navigating the Dashboard

Use the sidebar to access:
- **Profile**: Your personal information and settings
- **Donations**: Full donation history and receipts
- **Volunteering**: Your volunteer applications and hour logs
- **Settings**: Account preferences and notifications
