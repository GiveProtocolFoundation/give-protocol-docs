## Sign Up

1. Go to [app.giveprotocol.io](https://app.giveprotocol.io)
2. Click **Get Started** or **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email address
4. Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
5. Accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
6. Click **Create Account**

## Email Verification

After signing up, you will receive a verification email. Click the link in the email to verify your address.

> **Didn't receive the email?** Check your spam folder, or use the **Resend Email** button on the verification page.

## Account Settings

Once verified, access your account settings to:

- Update your display name and profile photo
- Set notification preferences
- Manage connected wallets
- Configure privacy settings

## Security Recommendations

- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) in account settings
- Use a unique, strong password
- Never share your wallet seed phrase
- Sign out of shared devices after use
