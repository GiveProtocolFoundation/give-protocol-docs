## General Questions

### What is Give Protocol?

Give Protocol is a blockchain-based charitable giving platform that enables transparent, low-cost donations and volunteer coordination. All donations are recorded on the Moonbeam blockchain for full accountability.

### Is Give Protocol free to use?

Give Protocol charges a 2.5% platform fee on donations. There are no fees for browsing, creating an account, or volunteering. Gas fees (network fees) for blockchain transactions are separate and paid to the network.

### Which cryptocurrencies can I donate?

Currently supported: **GLMR** (Moonbeam's native token). Support for additional tokens is planned.

### Is Give Protocol available worldwide?

Yes. Give Protocol has no geographical restrictions. Anyone with an internet connection and a crypto wallet can participate as a donor or volunteer. Organizations must complete our verification process, which may have regional requirements.

## Donations

### How do I make a donation?

1. Find an organization or campaign
2. Click **Donate**
3. Enter the amount
4. Confirm in your wallet
5. Receive a blockchain receipt

### Can I get a receipt for tax purposes?

Yes. Each donation generates a blockchain transaction record. For formal tax documentation, contact the receiving organization directly, as tax treatment varies by jurisdiction.

### Can I donate anonymously?

Yes. Enable **Anonymous Donations** in your Privacy Settings. Your donation will still be recorded on the blockchain, but your profile name will not appear on the organization's public donor list.

### Can I set up recurring donations?

Yes. When making a donation, check the **Recurring** option and set your preferred frequency (weekly, monthly, quarterly).

## Organizations

### How do I register my organization?

Navigate to **Register Organization** in your account. You will need to provide:
- Organization name and legal documents
- Mission statement
- Wallet address for receiving donations
- Contact information

Verification typically takes 3–5 business days.

### What organizations are eligible?

We accept registered non-profits, charities, and social enterprises. All organizations undergo identity verification before being listed on the platform.

## Volunteers

### How does volunteer hour verification work?

Volunteer supervisors at the organization can verify hours directly on the platform. Verified hours are recorded on-chain and contribute to your Impact Score.

### Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes. Many organizations offer remote volunteering opportunities. Filter opportunities by **Remote** when browsing.

## Technical

### Which wallets are supported?

MetaMask, WalletConnect-compatible wallets, and Coinbase Wallet. See [Wallet Connection](/getting-started/wallet-connection/) for setup instructions.

### Why is my transaction failing?

Common reasons:
- Insufficient gas (GLMR) — add more GLMR to your wallet
- Network congestion — try again in a few minutes
- Wrong network — switch to Moonbeam in your wallet

### Is Give Protocol secure?

Our smart contracts have been audited by independent security firms. All code is open source. We follow industry best practices for security. See [Platform Security](/safety-security/platform-security/) for details.
